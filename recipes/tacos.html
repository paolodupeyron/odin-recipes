Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made by folding a soft or hard tortilla around a filling. The fillings can range from grilled meats like chicken, beef, or pork to vegetarian options such as beans, cheese, or grilled vegetables. Common toppings include fresh ingredients like lettuce, onions, tomatoes, cilantro, and salsa, as well as garnishes like guacamole and sour cream. Tacos are highly versatile and are typically served as street food or a casual meal.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas (corn or flour)
  • Grilled chicken, beef, pork, or vegetables
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole
  • Sour cream
  • Lettuce
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Lime (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Grill or cook the chicken, beef, pork, or vegetables until fully cooked.
  2. Warm tortillas in a pan or microwave.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing the cooked filling in the tortillas.
  4. Add toppings like onion, tomato, cilantro, lettuce, salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.
  5. Serve with a lime wedge on the side for extra flavor.
